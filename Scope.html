2.1 Scope and Limits

The present work is limited to venture capital investment operations, namely through venture capital funds, having as target companies, national companies.

There will be no in-depth analysis of the remaining venture capital and private equity operations, i.e., private equity, which includes, among other operations, restructuring, refinancing and financing operations for companies already consolidated, as in the case of expansion operations.

The financing through credit institutions, in particular by medium-long term loans, are excluded from this analysis due to the majority of these new companies do not have the minimum requirements for a favourable credit review (particularly the absence any assets or collateral to provide the credit institution2).
